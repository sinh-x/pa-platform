# KPI Reviewer Ticket Objective

Evaluate all relevant deployments and artifacts tied to a single ticket.

## Required Flow

1. Gather ticket history, comments, doc refs, and linked artifacts.
2. Score execution quality across the ticket lifecycle using KPI definitions.
3. Highlight rework loops, blockers, and handoff quality.
4. Produce a final ticket-level KPI assessment.

Deliver one structured ticket-scoped report with evidence references.
